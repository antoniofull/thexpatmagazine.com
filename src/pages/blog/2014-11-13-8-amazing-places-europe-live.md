**Europe** is big, full of history and with many, many beautiful places. Some of them are really famous, who doesn&#8217;t know about Paris? But there are some magical, secret places that you probably have not heard so much. If you are considering moving to a place where the quality of life is higher and lifestyle is calm, then you should consider one of the place in this list.

## 1 &#8211; Sotogrande.

<img class="size-large wp-image-230" src="/img/uploads/2014/11/3877048267_c722e5d083_o-1024x649.jpg" alt="3877048267_c722e5d083_o" width="785" height="497" srcset="/img/uploads/2014/11/3877048267_c722e5d083_o-1024x649.jpg 1024w, /img/uploads/2014/11/3877048267_c722e5d083_o-300x190.jpg 300w, /img/uploads/2014/11/3877048267_c722e5d083_o-768x487.jpg 768w, /img/uploads/2014/11/3877048267_c722e5d083_o-788x500.jpg 788w, /img/uploads/2014/11/3877048267_c722e5d083_o.jpg 1200w" sizes="(max-width: 785px) 100vw, 785px" />

Foto : <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/druidabruxux/3877048267/in/photolist-6UATnM-5wgnnG-9eDUed-73SEEA-73NHbz-su5eP-5GEzVy-9bbJtr-6bwDtK-su5dK-7ekkQM-5w5tc1-8hC2Nz-6V8RDC-84qkBN-c8PopU-c8PnQ3-6V4LZi-c8Pm65-akYsvt-6UASHe-5UnNSP-5UnNXT-5Usb3s-5UnP9i-5UnP3Z-5UnNCn-5UnNhR-5UnNwD-5UsaHJ-5UnNnR-9eDRJW-9eDT1W-c8Pn83-c8PkD1-c8PmBQ-c8PkeJ-c8PjXC-6UEWME-4d6BKp-4daCvy-4HyTQg-6V4Lbx-6V4MX6-4HD3LC-4Hqzj5-9eALV2-6UATZP-6UATak-6V8RSo" target="_blank">Flickr</a>

**Sotogrande** isa small town in the **south of Spain, in &#8220;La Costa del Sol&#8221;**, between **Malaga** and **Cadiz**.

**Golf** and **Polo** are the preferred sports and many of the house or building have a swimming pool.  Also, many V.I.P. have house here, like the former UK prime minister Tony Blair.

If you feel it too **cozy and calm**, you can drive an half hour and get to Malaga or **Marbella** for the _crazy night life._

## 2 &#8211; Reine, Norway

<img src="/img/uploads/2014/11/14418752586_da44015878_k-1024x576.jpg" alt="Reine, norway" width="785" height="441" srcset="/img/uploads/2014/11/14418752586_da44015878_k-1024x576.jpg 1024w, /img/uploads/2014/11/14418752586_da44015878_k-300x169.jpg 300w, /img/uploads/2014/11/14418752586_da44015878_k-768x432.jpg 768w, /img/uploads/2014/11/14418752586_da44015878_k-889x500.jpg 889w, /img/uploads/2014/11/14418752586_da44015878_k.jpg 1200w" sizes="(max-width: 785px) 100vw, 785px" />

**Colmar** is a medieval city founded around 1200, during the Roman Empire. Is called &#8220;_the little Venice_&#8221; because of the waterways that cross the town. Is also the capital of wine of **_Alsace_**.

_**Manarola**_ is a small town in the region of Liguria, Italy. In the famous and lovely **_&#8220;Cinque Terre&#8221;_**.

The colored _houses sorrounded by the rocks_ make look the town like in a painting. Is also famous for its superb wine and food. Must stop if you are visiting Italy.

## 5 &#8211; Cochem, Germany

<img class="size-large wp-image-240" src="/img/uploads/2014/11/cochem-germany-1024x680.jpg" alt="Cochem germany" width="785" height="521" />

Foto : <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/poly-image/14522103549/in/photolist-aBycop-dnvi6C-pYLZQ6-o8gzrF-hrRMHT-dnvioC-3Jzy3-kwbjx4-dYQDHq-dnvhgs-jXukWi-njEZrY-dnvhy7-fm1syr-nmKR6A-dnvdUp-nmr7MR-iBNZQe-hLNKc-hLiKTu-dCYnkf-a34UQA-fHfDfp-5mNuE3-dg4zPj-nCt9pF-eLHJkK-5mHAu9-eY9K7j-6xqxcr-4JxXHm-6boa9J-g8ujJv-dLeCeC-9moE5H-fKEV7B-7sQgxA-nGJMUT-9ZTmoe-hAdR4G-hAdFvc-hAetbQ-hAewyb-d9UUUS-fPeEdi-8Gp3sG-faZnwm-5w2Xws-dCShLa-faZmWY" target="_blank">Flickr</a>

Cochem is a traditional German town located Rhineland-Palatinate, Germany. With just 5000 inhabitants is one of the smallest town in Germany. Tourism anyway is strong because of the wonderful location and history. The Castle of Cochem is a must-see.

## 10 &#8211; Albarracin, Spain

<img class="size-full wp-image-250" src="/img/uploads/2014/11/3556646432_aa0df4fb8f_b.jpg" alt="Albarracin" width="1024" height="680" srcset="/img/uploads/2014/11/3556646432_aa0df4fb8f_b.jpg 1024w, /img/uploads/2014/11/3556646432_aa0df4fb8f_b-300x199.jpg 300w, /img/uploads/2014/11/3556646432_aa0df4fb8f_b-768x510.jpg 768w, /img/uploads/2014/11/3556646432_aa0df4fb8f_b-753x500.jpg 753w" sizes="(max-width: 1024px) 100vw, 1024px" />

Foto : <a href="https://www.flickr.com/photos/reinante/3556646432" target="_blank">Flickr</a>

**Albarracin** is a very small town, 1075 inhabitants, in the &#8220;Region Autonoma de Aragon&#8221;, in Spain. **Albarracin** si completely unspoilt by modernization and it feels like is still living in the middle age. For many is considered the most beautiful village in Spain, and we couldn&#8217;t agree more.
